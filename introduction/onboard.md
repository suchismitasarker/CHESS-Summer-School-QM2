# CHESS Summer Student Onboarding

Welcome to the **CHESS Summer Program for Quantum Materials Beamline (QM2)**!  
This guide outlines the required steps and resources to ensure a smooth onboarding process for all students.

---

## ✅ Account Setup
CLASSE IDs and BeamPASS logins are created by the IT group before students arrive.  
Please ensure you can log in to both systems **before your first day**.

---

## 📅 CHESS User Meeting
All students are expected to attend the CHESS User Meeting.  
This meeting provides important information about facility usage and expectations.

## 🧪 BeamPASS – Instructions for Wilson Lab Students

Students in the Wilson Lab must complete CHESS User training and quizzes via BeamPASS to receive monthly radiation badges.

### Step 1: Create a CLASSE ID (if needed)
1. Visit the BeamPASS login page  
2. Click **“Don’t have a CLASSE ID?”**  
3. Fill out the form (name + email)  
4. You will receive:
   - A confirmation email  
   - A follow-up email with your CLASSE ID and password setup link  

⚠️ *Important: Complete this step promptly, as manual processing may be required.*

---

### Step 2: Log in to BeamPASS
- Log in using your CLASSE ID and password  
- Update your personal record  
- For login issues, contact:  
  - service-classe@cornell.edu  
  - chessuseroffice@cornell.edu  

---

### Step 3: Complete Training
- Review:
  - User Orientation & Safety Training  
  - User Guide  

- Complete the required exams:
  - ✅ **In-Person User Exam**  
  - ✅ **CHESS User Exam**
